Spirit's blessing

And thus, you begin your journey. As you trudge your way to the castle, you encounter an obstacle. Your vision starts to blur from fatigue and you start to lose feelings in your feet. But the show must go on. So despite this, you travel on. As you continue to walk, you discover a fork in the road. You discover two paths. On the left, a dark and mysterious forest that makes you shiver in fear. On the right, a seemingly deserted road that shows no sign of life.
