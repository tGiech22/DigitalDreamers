The world is in danger!

You're the leader of the most renown country and live a luxurious life within the marble walls and gorgeous trees. However out of nowhere, tragedy occurs and there's an apocalypse coming. Suddenly your once beloved area is burning in flames. This leaves everyone with no choice but to flee. As the leader of your country, your task is to live up to your responsibility of protecting your land. Follow through the story map to prevent any damage and protect your land from the vicious dragons... Good luck!
