To flee or not to flee

You decide to sprint away as fast as possible. Unfortunately for you, while you were too busy thinking about fleeing, the bandits circled you. You're unable to dodge them in time and get a hit straight in the face...Bull's eye! That black eye doesn't seem like it's going to be going anywhere anytime soon and neither are the bandits. You have no choice but to try and stand your ground until they disappear into the night. You are badly bruised.
