You're all alone with nothing in sight...

You end up roaming what seems like a deserted road with nothing but your own self-- no source of light, no safety device, not even a sense of direction. So far, you've kept calm and plan on continuing to do so. Taking small steps at a time, you make your way forward. All you hear are your footsteps below you... or so you thought. From the distance, you hear eary cackling and you freeze. The laughter approaches you and you see a short but mighty looking witch with an evil smirk coming your way. Your two choices are to interact with the witch or ignore and walk around her, pretending you never even noticed her.

Which do you choose?
